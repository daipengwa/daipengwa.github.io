Peng Dai

Peng Dai is a fourth-year Ph.D. student at CVMI Lab, Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The University of Hong Kong (HKU). His supervisor is Prof. Xiaojuan Qi. His research interest includes computer vision and computer graphics. Recently, he focuses on neural rendering and 3D/Video generation. He also works closely with Dr. Yinda Zhang and Dr. Zhuwen Li.

Before that, he obtained his Master and Bachelor degrees from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2020 and 2017, respectively, advised by Prof. Shuaicheng Liu and Prof. Bing Zeng.
