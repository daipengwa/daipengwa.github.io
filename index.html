Peng Dai is a second year PhD candidate at University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xiaojuan Qi. Before this, he obtained his Master and Bachelor degrees from UESTC in 2020 and 2017,respectively, and was supervised by Prof. Bing Zeng and Prof. Shuaicheng Liu. During his master study, he was lucky to work with Dr. Zhuwen Li and Dr. Yinda Zhang. His research interests include deep learning, couputer vision and neural rendering.
